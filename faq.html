Frequently Asked Questions

What ages can join?

We welcome dancers of all ages above 5 years old. Adult students are welcome to join as well!

Do I need prior experience?

No experience is required. Batches of students are always grouped by level.

What styles do you teach?

We offer dance lessons in Bharatanatyam.

How often are lessons?

We operate on a semester schedule with each semester containing 10 classes. The 1st semester typically operates from fall until the end of winte and the 2nd semester runs from the start of spring to summer. Classes may be adjusted or added based on circumstances.

How much to lessons cost?

Our pricing is $100 per semester ($10 per class).
